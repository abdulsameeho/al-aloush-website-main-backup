Services

We deliver Business Support Sponsorship Services, Governmental Services, Heterogonous Support Services & Real Estate Services & Lighting Solutions for Government Sectors.

Corporate Sponsorship

We focus on assisting multinational corporates to build its presence by offering them all professional registration services that are essential to create a legal entity in Kuwait as well as other countries in the Middle east region. The services involve activities related to obtain the ownership legally empowering the customer to operate their business seamlessly in the respective country.

Corporate Governmental

We provide all the governmental services related to register to avail the company registration processes at all government authorities. This involves Corporate Entity, Trade Activity, Commercial, Municipality, Civil Information, Fire Department etc. Registration processes.

Business Support

We actively engage and assist our customers to support the customer's business for its routine operation. This support services comprise Work permit or Visit Visa Services for business owners and employees, supply of Skilled or unskilled Human Resource, Office workspace Rentals or leasing services, Office workspace fixtures, Corporate Telecommunication Services, Corporate Cellular Services, Corporate Internet Services, Corporate Insurance Services, Procurement & Logistics Services, Legal Consultancy & Support Services, Corporate Housing Services, Vehicle short & long Term Rental Leasing services.

Real Estate
